# PropAdvisor CL — Competitive Benchmark

_Last updated: May 6, 2026_

## Overview

This document maps every relevant competitor in the Chilean real estate market, evaluates their strengths/weaknesses, and identifies where PropAdvisor has an advantage or a gap.

---

## 1. Portal Inmobiliario (portalinmobiliario.com)

**Owner:** Mercado Libre (MELI, $30B+ market cap)
**Type:** Property listing marketplace
**Market position:** Dominant portal. 90%+ of online property listings in Chile.

### What they offer
- Property listings (sale + rent) across all of Chile
- Filters: city, comuna, rooms, price, m², type
- Featured projects from inmobiliarias (paid placements)
- "Proyectos destacados" carousel (sponsored)
- Corredora/inmobiliaria storefronts
- Blog with real estate tips
- Online reservation for new projects (Mercado Libre Inmuebles integration)
- Seasonal rental (arriendo de temporada)

### What they DON'T offer
- ❌ No mortgage calculator
- ❌ No buy vs rent comparison
- ❌ No financial analysis (dividendo, IRR, cap rate)
- ❌ No bank rate comparison
- ❌ No UF-adjusted projections
- ❌ No lead qualification or scoring
- ❌ No investment analysis tools

### Revenue model
- Listing fees for corredoras/inmobiliarias (plans from ~CLP $50K-$500K/month)
- Featured/promoted placements
- Inmobiliaria subscription packages
- Part of MELI ecosystem (payments, shipping, fintech)

### PropAdvisor vs Portal Inmobiliario
| Dimension | Portal Inmobiliario | PropAdvisor |
|-----------|-------------------|-------------|
| Property listings | ✅ 100K+ listings | ❌ ~100 mock properties |
| Financial analysis | ❌ None | ✅ 3-scenario, 20-year |
| Bank comparison | ❌ None | ✅ 8 banks, live rates |
| Buy vs rent decision | ❌ None | ✅ Core product |
| Investment metrics | ❌ None | ✅ IRR, DSCR, cap rate |
| Lead quality | Low (browsing intent) | High (analysis intent) |
| Target user | Browsers | Decision-makers |

**Takeaway:** Not a competitor — a potential partner. Portal users find properties; PropAdvisor users analyze them. Integration opportunity (white-label calculator on Portal listings).

## 2. TocToc (toctoc.com)

**Type:** Real estate data + analytics platform
**Market position:** Premium data provider, strong B2B presence

### What they offer
- Market reports and analytics (price trends, transaction data)
- Property valuation estimates (AVM — automated valuation model)
- Comparable sales data (transacciones CBR)
- Neighborhood/comuna analysis
- B2B data products for banks, inmobiliarias, corredoras
- Property listings (smaller than Portal Inmobiliario)
- "Informe de mercado" per zona
- Blog and research content

### What they DON'T offer
- ❌ No mortgage calculator or dividendo simulator
- ❌ No buy vs rent comparison
- ❌ No lead generation for brokers (they ARE the data, not the funnel)
- ❌ No free user-facing analysis tool
- ❌ Limited investment analysis (no IRR, DSCR)
- ❌ No multi-bank rate comparison

### Revenue model
- B2B data subscriptions (banks, inmobiliarias, corredoras)
- Property reports (paid per report or subscription)
- API access for lenders and platforms
- Sponsored content and brand partnerships

### PropAdvisor vs TocToc
| Dimension | TocToc | PropAdvisor |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| Market data depth | ✅ Deep (CBR transactions, historical) | ⚠️ Limited (comuna-level, no CBR) |
| Property valuation | ✅ AVM | ❌ Not offered |
| Financial analysis | ❌ None | ✅ Complete (3 scenarios) |
| Free tool for consumers | ❌ No | ✅ Core product |
| B2B data products | ✅ Strong | ❌ Not yet (planned Year 2) |
| Lead generation | ❌ Not their model | ✅ Core revenue |

**Takeaway:** TocToc is data-focused B2B. PropAdvisor is analysis-focused B2C. Different markets. Could be a data source partner (buy comuna-level data from TocToc to enrich PropAdvisor analysis). Long-term competitor if PropAdvisor enters B2B data.

## 3. Propital (propital.com)

**Type:** Real estate investment advisory (full-service)
**Market position:** Premium investment advisory for Chilean investors

### What they offer
- Personalized investment advisory (human consultants)
- Curated developer projects (pre-vetted departments for rent)
- Investment simulator (pie, plusvalía, arriendo, rate, horizon)
- AI-powered market analysis tools
- Projected patrimony visualization (5, 10, 20 years)
- Full-service: from selection → purchase → escrituración → first rent
- Lead capture: name, phone, capital available, monthly income, investment experience
- Blog with investment content

### Key metrics (from their site)
- 3,000+ active investors
- $300M+ capital managed
- 32.4% claimed average annual ROI (arriendo + plusvalía)
- 99.8% client satisfaction
- 10+ years in market, 5 countries

### What they DON'T offer
- ❌ No free analysis tool (requires human consultation)
- ❌ No multi-bank rate comparison
- ❌ No "buy vs rent" decision (only "buy to invest")
- ❌ No self-serve (everything goes through a sales rep)
- ❌ No first-time homebuyer focus
- ❌ No transparency on fees/pricing (advisory fee structure unclear)

### Revenue model
- Advisory/consulting fees
- Developer commissions (referral fees from inmobiliarias)
- Management fees on investment portfolios
- Likely earns CLP $500K-$3M per client engagement

### PropAdvisor vs Propital
| Dimension | Propital | PropAdvisor |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Target audience | Sophisticated investors ($300M+) | Mass market (first-time buyers + small investors) |
| Service model | Full-service human advisory | Self-serve tool |
| Price | Expensive (advisor fees) | Free (tool) + $15K/mo Pro |
| Investment analysis | ✅ Deep, personalized | ✅ Self-serve, automated |
| Buy vs rent decision | ❌ Only investment | ✅ 3 scenarios |
| Lead capture quality | ✅ Excellent (capital, income, experience) | ✅ Good (phone, income, pre-approval) |
| Accessibility | ❌ High barrier (consultation required) | ✅ Zero barrier (free, no account) |
| Bank comparison | ❌ None | ✅ 8 banks |
| Scale | ❌ Limited by human advisors | ✅ Unlimited (software) |

**Takeaway:** Propital is the premium end of the market PropAdvisor targets. PropAdvisor captures the 80% of buyers/investors who will never pay for a Propital consultation. Propital's lead capture form is a GREAT benchmark for field design (capital available, experience level, investment objective). Not a direct competitor — different price point and service model.

## PropAdvisor's Unique Position

**No platform in Chile combines ALL of these:**
1. Free, multi-bank mortgage calculator (8 banks)
2. 3-scenario buy vs rent comparison with 20-year projections
3. Comuna-level appreciation and cap rate data (60+ locations)
4. Investment-grade metrics (IRR, DSCR, cash-on-cash)
5. Automated lead scoring and broker routing
6. PDF + Excel professional reports
7. Zero registration required for core product

**PropAdvisor's moat is the intersection of free analysis + lead generation.** Every competitor is either:
- A listing portal (Portal Inmobiliario, GoPlaceIt) — no financial analysis
- A data provider (TocToc) — no consumer-facing free tool
- A premium advisory (Propital) — high barrier, limited scale
- A generic calculator (Rankia, bank sites) — single scenario, no comparison
- A comparison site (ComparaBien) — too broad, no depth

## Learnings from Competitors (What to Steal)

### From Propital
- **Lead capture fields:** They ask for capital available, monthly income, and investment experience. PropAdvisor should add "investment experience" as an optional field — it helps brokers tailor their pitch.
- **"32.4% ROI" claim:** Bold social proof. PropAdvisor should show aggregate portfolio performance data when available.
- **WhatsApp integration:** Propital uses WhatsApp as primary contact. ✅ Already added to broker emails.

### From Portal Inmobiliario
- **"Proyectos destacados" carousel:** Direct model for PropAdvisor's OpportunitiesView. When real developer deals are signed, this becomes a revenue feature.
- **Corredora storefronts:** Could model a "broker profile" page for PropAdvisor's partner brokers.

### From Rankia
- **Community forums:** PropAdvisor has zero community. A simple "pregunta al experto" section could drive SEO traffic and engagement.
- **Content volume:** Rankia has hundreds of articles. PropAdvisor has 1 guide. More long-form content = more LLM citations.

### From TocToc
- **Transaction data (CBR):** If PropAdvisor could access Conservador de Bienes Raíces data, it would transform the valuation product from estimates to facts.
- **Market reports per zona:** Potential premium product (monthly comuna reports).
