# PropAdvisor CL — Business Model

## What Is This

A free web tool that helps Chileans decide whether to buy or rent property. Users input their budget, choose a property, and get a 20-year financial comparison across three scenarios with real bank rates and live UF data.

**One-line pitch:** PropAdvisor is the first platform in Chile that calculates whether buying or renting is the better financial decision — for free, in under 5 minutes.

---

## Who Uses It

| Segment | Trigger | Size |
|---|---|---|
| First-time buyers (28–40 yrs) | Thinking about buying their first home | ~400K people/year |
| Investors | Evaluating whether a property generates positive cash flow | ~80K people/year |
| Renters | Wondering if now is the right time to buy | ~1.2M people in major cities |

**Primary user:** Middle-class Chilean, 28–45 years old, household income CLP $2M–$6M/month, considering their first property purchase in Santiago, Valparaíso, or Concepción.

---

## Revenue Streams

### Stream 1 — Affiliate commissions (first revenue, 6–12 months)

When a user decides to buy, they need a mortgage. PropAdvisor refers them to a bank or mortgage broker.

| Referral type | Commission | Typical deal size | Revenue per conversion |
|---|---|---|---|
| Bank mortgage origination | 0.3–0.8% of loan | ~UF 3,000 (~$110M CLP) | **CLP $330K–$880K per deal** |
| Real estate broker | 1–2% of property price | ~$130M CLP | **CLP $1.3M–$2.6M per deal** |
| Mortgage life/property insurance | Flat referral fee | — | **CLP $30K–$80K per policy** |

**To make CLP $10M/month:** 12–30 mortgage referral conversions. At 5% conversion from analysis to referral click, that requires ~240–600 analyses/month — reachable in Month 3.

**Key action needed:** Sign affiliate agreement with at least one mortgage broker or bank before launch. BancoEstado and CMR Falabella both run referral programs.

---

### Stream 2 — Premium PDF reports (Month 6+)

Users who want to share their analysis with a bank, a partner, or a notary will pay for a professional-grade export.

- **Price:** CLP $4,900 per report (~$5 USD)
- **Conversion target:** 8–12% of users who complete an analysis
- **Revenue at 500 analyses/month:** CLP $196K–$294K/month

No subscription required. Single-transaction, impulse purchase at the moment of highest intent.

---

### Stream 3 — B2B white-label API (Year 2+)

Real estate portals (Portal Inmobiliario, Portalinmobiliario.com) and banks could embed PropAdvisor's calculator directly in their listings.

- **Price:** CLP $500K–$2M/month per integration
- **Target:** 2–3 clients generates CLP $1M–$6M/month recurring
- **Trigger:** Once we have traffic data to show (>10K monthly analyses)

2. **Get affiliate agreement signed** with one broker or bank (target: first 30 days post-launch)
3. **Drive 500 analyses** through SEO + social (target: first 60 days)
4. **Convert 5% to referral clicks** = 25 qualified leads sent to affiliate partner
5. **Close 3–5 deals** at avg. CLP $500K commission = **CLP $1.5M–$2.5M**

## Competitive Moat

No Chilean platform currently offers a free, real-data, 20-year comparison across all three scenarios (buy to live, buy to rent, keep renting). Portals like Portal Inmobiliario show listings but have no financial analysis. Banks show only their own mortgage calculator. PropAdvisor is the only neutral, multi-scenario tool.

**Defensibility:** Data quality (real UF + real bank rates), SEO depth (LLM-optimized content), and brand trust built through giving away real analysis for free.

---

## What This Business Is NOT

- Not a real estate portal (we don't list properties for sale)
- Not a bank or lender (we don't originate credit)
- Not a subscription SaaS (premium features are one-time transactions)
- Not dependent on advertising revenue (affiliate model is far more aligned with user intent)
