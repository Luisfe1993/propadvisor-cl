# PropAdvisor CL — Business Model

## What Is This

A free web tool that helps Chileans decide whether to buy or rent property. Users input their budget, choose a property, and get a 20-year financial comparison across three scenarios with real bank rates and live UF data.

**One-line pitch:** PropAdvisor is the first platform in Chile that calculates whether buying or renting is the better financial decision — for free, in under 5 minutes.

---

## Who Uses It

| Segment | Trigger | Size |
|---|---|---|
| First-time buyers (28–40 yrs) | Thinking about buying their first home | ~400K people/year |
| Investors | Evaluating whether a property generates positive cash flow | ~80K people/year |
| Renters | Wondering if now is the right time to buy | ~1.2M people in major cities |

**Primary user:** Middle-class Chilean, 28–45 years old, household income CLP $2M–$6M/month, considering their first property purchase in Santiago, Valparaíso, or Concepción.

---

## The Real Asset

PropAdvisor's value is not the calculator itself — it's the **email + full financial profile of high-intent buyers**. Every person who enters their email has identified a specific property, defined their budget, chosen a down payment percentage, and expressed intent to buy. That is the highest-quality lead in Chilean real estate.

The tool is free because giving away the analysis builds trust and captures the lead. Revenue comes from what happens with that lead afterward.

---

## Revenue Streams

### Stream 1 — Qualified Lead Sales to Mortgage Brokers (First Revenue — Month 4–6)

Don't try to be an affiliate. Be a **lead generator.**

We already collect email + full financial profile (property price, down payment, city, property type, implied income). A mortgage broker in Chile pays **CLP $15K–$50K per qualified lead** — someone who has identified a property, knows their budget, and has expressed intent to buy.

- No attribution to the sale required. We sell the lead. What the broker does with it is their problem.
- Requires **opt-in consent**: "¿Quieres que un ejecutivo hipotecario te contacte?" checkbox or step after the analysis.
- At 500 analyses/month, 15% opt-in = 75 leads × CLP $25K = **CLP $1.87M/month**.
- We need **one broker agreement**, not four bank partnerships.

**Why this works and affiliates don't:**
- Chilean banks don't run open affiliate programs for random websites.
- Affiliate attribution requires tracked links AND bank-side conversion reporting — infrastructure that doesn't exist in Chile's banking market.
- Lead sales are a well-understood model. Brokers already buy leads from Facebook, Google, and cold-call centers. We offer better quality at lower cost.

**Action needed:** Sign agreement with one mortgage broker before Month 3. Deliver first batch of 20–50 leads as a free trial to prove quality.

---

### Stream 2 — Sponsored Bank Placement (Month 6–12)

Instead of affiliate links, sell **visibility** to banks.

- "BancoEstado aparece como banco recomendado en todos los análisis" — CLP $500K–$2M/month flat fee.
- Banks already spend millions on Google Ads for "crédito hipotecario Chile." We offer a user who is **in the middle of deciding to buy** with a specific property in mind. That's worth more than a search ad click.
- This is native advertising aligned with the user's interest — not banner ads.
- Requires traffic data to sell: 1,000+ analyses/month minimum.

| Clients | Monthly fee | Monthly revenue |
|---|---|---|
| 1 bank | CLP $500K–$1M | CLP $500K–$1M |
| 2–3 banks (rotating placement) | CLP $500K–$1M each | CLP $1M–$3M |

### Stream 3 — Real Estate Developer Featured Projects (Month 6–12)

Target **inmobiliarias** (developers selling new projects), not individual property owners.

- Developers spend CLP $5M–$50M/month on marketing per project.
- Offer: "Your new development appears in PropAdvisor search results for [neighborhood]. Users run the buy-vs-rent analysis on your project. You get the lead."
- Price: CLP $200K–$1M/month per featured project.
- This is a performance-marketing product, not a listing service. The value is the **analysis context** — the user sees "buying this specific unit in your project is $X cheaper than renting over 20 years."
- No need to track the sale. The developer pays for featured placement, like a Google Ad.

---

### Stream 4 — Email Nurture Sequence (Month 2+, near-zero cost)

We have emails of people actively considering buying. Monetize the relationship over time:

- **Week 1:** Analysis recap + "tasas bajaron esta semana" rate update
- **Week 2:** Related properties in their target area
- **Week 3:** Sponsored content from a mortgage broker or insurance company
- **Ongoing:** Monthly market updates with sponsored placement

This turns a one-time visitor into recurring engagement and multiplies the value of every lead. A single email list of 5,000 high-intent buyers is worth CLP $500K–$2M/month in sponsored sends to a broker or insurer.

---

### Stream 5 — B2B White-label API (Year 2+)

Real estate portals (Portal Inmobiliario) and banks embed PropAdvisor's calculator directly in their listings.

- Price: CLP $500K–$2M/month per integration.
- The pitch: "Add a 'Run financial analysis' button on every listing. We handle the calculator. You keep the traffic. Revenue share on leads generated."
- Requires proven traffic data (>10K monthly analyses) and a legal entity.
- Portal Inmobiliario could build this internally, so the play is to prove the model with traffic before they do.

## Why Not Affiliates, Paid PDFs, or Property Listings

### Affiliates won't work in Chile
Chilean banks don't run open affiliate programs. There's no tracking infrastructure. Even with a deal, proving attribution requires the bank to report conversions back — they won't. At realistic click-through rates (<1%) and close rates (5%), 500 analyses/month produces 0.25 deals. That's zero revenue.

### Paid PDF reports won't cover hosting
The free web analysis already shows everything. Industry freemium conversion is 2–5%. At 500 analyses × 3% × CLP $4,900 = CLP $73,500/month ($75 USD). Dead on arrival.

### "List free, pay on conversion" for property owners won't work
Attribution is impossible — buyers contact sellers directly, negotiate offline, close at a notaría. No way to prove or charge. Also creates a chicken-and-egg problem (no traffic = no sellers, no sellers = no traffic) and turns us into a marketplace competitor to Portal Inmobiliario, a division of a $30B company. Not our fight.

---

## Competitive Moat

No Chilean platform offers a free, real-data, 20-year comparison across all three scenarios (buy to live, buy to rent, keep renting). Portals show listings but no financial analysis. Banks show only their own calculator. PropAdvisor is the only neutral, multi-scenario tool.

**Defensibility:** Data quality (real UF + real bank rates), SEO depth (LLM-optimized content), brand trust from giving away real analysis for free, and the growing email list of high-intent buyers.

---

## What This Business Is NOT

- Not a real estate portal (we don't list properties for sale)
- Not a bank or lender (we don't originate credit)
- Not a marketplace (we don't connect buyers and sellers)
- Not dependent on affiliate tracking infrastructure that doesn't exist in Chile
- Not dependent on advertising revenue (lead sales are aligned with user intent)
